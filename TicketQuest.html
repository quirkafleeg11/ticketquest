TicketQuest

Long ago in ages past, the ancestors knew of a magic called 'tickets'. These tickets granted them great wisdom of who was troubled. The ancestors used tickets to help those in need, and they became known as the Supporting Ones, as they roamed the lands on a quest to assist. Now, this magic has returned and must be used by you, the chosen one, to assist once more. Welcome to TicketQuest.

A simple levelling system to make closing tickets feel worth it. Each ticket completed, click the relevent button by priority. Click level up once you reach 50 XP.
